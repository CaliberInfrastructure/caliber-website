## 2. TARGET AUDIENCE

### Primary Audience: Med Spa Owners/Operators
**Demographics:**
- Med spa monthly revenue: $30,000–$400,000
- Staff size: 3–15 employees
- Locations: 1–3 locations
- Decision maker: Owner-operator (fast decisions, direct relationship)

**Psychographics:**
- Pain-aware: Knows they're losing revenue to operational inefficiencies
- Growth-minded but resource-constrained
- Frustrated with missed calls, no-shows, slow follow-ups
- Seeking operational stability and scalability
- Values ROI and measurable results
- Skeptical of "marketing agencies" promising vague results

**Key Pain Points:**
1. 15–25 missed calls per week that never convert
2. 20–30% no-show rates costing $6,000+/month
3. Slow inquiry response times (4–24 hours avg) losing leads to competitors
4. 40% of inquiries come after 6pm with zero response until next day
5. 30–50% of past clients haven't returned in 90+ days (dormant database)

**Estimated Monthly Revenue Loss:** $25,000–$35,000 per typical med spa

## 8. DESIGN DIRECTION & AESTHETIC

### Overall Aesthetic
- **Professional, Modern, Clean** – Not flashy, not generic agency vibes
- **Tech-Forward but Accessible** – Sophisticated without being cold
- **Trust-Building** – Medical-adjacent professionalism (clean, organized, credible)
- **South Florida Market Fit** – Upscale but not overly corporate

### Visual Inspiration Keywords
- "SaaS platform meets professional services"
- "Enterprise software simplified"
- "Medical technology meets automation"
- "Confident minimalism"

### Color Palette Considerations
- **Primary:** Deep blue or navy (trust, professionalism, technology)
- **Accent:** Teal or electric blue (modern, AI-forward)
- **Neutral:** White, light gray, charcoal (clean, readable)
- **Avoid:** Overly bright colors, gradients that feel "startup-y", medical reds

### Typography
- **Headings:** Bold, modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Satoshi, Manrope)
- **Body:** Clean, readable sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Open Sans, System UI)
- **Accent/Data:** Monospace for numbers/metrics (optional, for credibility)

### Imagery Style
- **Hero Images:** High-quality med spa interiors (modern, clean, aspirational)
- **Icons:** Simple, line-based or minimal solid icons for features
- **Avoid:** Stock photos of generic business people, overly clinical medical imagery
- **Consider:** Abstract tech visuals (subtle), dashboard mockups, workflow diagrams
